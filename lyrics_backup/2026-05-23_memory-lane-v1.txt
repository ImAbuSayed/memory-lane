[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
We park right here beneath the neon sign,
Just like we did back in the summertime.
You trace my collar with a velvet touch,
I never knew I could want you this much.

[Verse 1]
We're reminiscing on the hood tonight,
Your skin is glowing in the amber light.
You laugh about the way we used to be,
But all I'm seeing is your eyes on me.

[Pre-Chorus]
Every memory is fanning the flame,
Making me remember why I call your name.
The years between us are melting away,
There’s only one thing left for me to say.

[Chorus]
Let's take a midnight drive down memory lane,
Where every touch is washing away the pain.
I want to trace the curves I've always known,
And make you feel like you are coming home.

[Post-Chorus]
Down memory lane, where the feeling is strong,
Right here beside you is where I belong.

[Verse 2]
I feel the tremble of your gentle sigh,
The spark of magic when you catch my eye.
My leather jacket wrapped around your dress,
I'm getting lost inside your sweet caress.

[Pre-Chorus 2]
Every memory is fanning the flame,
Making me remember why I call your name.
The years between us are melting away,
There’s only one thing left for me to say.

[Chorus]
Let's take a midnight drive down memory lane,
Where every touch is washing away the pain.
I want to trace the curves I've always known,
And make you feel like you are coming home.

[Post-Chorus 2]
Down memory lane, where the feeling is strong,
Right here beside you is where I belong.

[Bridge]
The shadows fall but the fire is bright,
I see my destiny in the silence tonight.
A beating heart pulling us so near,
The only echo that I want to hear.
I hold this feeling till the morning light,
Say my name softly, Saeed tonight [Whispered] (Saeed... Saeed...)

[Spoken]
I've been waiting my whole life to find this feeling again.
Just you and me, letting the world fade away.

[Final Chorus]
Let's take a midnight drive down memory lane,
Where every kiss is washing away the pain.
I'm gonna love you like I've always known,
Right here with me is where you're coming home.
